HALO-HALO

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢crushed or shaved ice
  • Ingredient 2 ▢milk (fresh or evaporated)
  • Ingredient 3 ▢sugar, if needed
  • Ingredient 4 ▢sweetened saba or plantain bananas
  • Ingredient 5 ▢fresh or sweetened langka (jack fruit)
  • Ingredient 6 ▢sweetened sweet potatoes
  • Ingredient 7 ▢sweetened garbanzos or beans
  • Ingredient 8 ▢coconut strips or sweetened macapuno
  • Ingredient 9 ▢sweetened red munggo
  • Ingredient 10 ▢nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • Ingredient 11 ▢sago or tapioca pearls or jelly cut into cubes pinipig
  • Ingredient 12 ▢leche flan (topping)
  • Ingredient 13 ▢ube jam or ube ice cream (topping)

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 In a tall glass, add a teaspoon of each of your selected ingredients.
  2. Step 2 Add sugar if you like.
  3. Step 3 Fill the glass with shaved ice up to the brim.
  4. Step 4 Drizzle with milk.
  5. Step 5 Add the toppings of choice.
  6. Step 6 Serve with a long spoon to mix ('halo') and enjoy.

BUKO SALAD

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢4 cups young coconut buko, shredded
  • Ingredient 2 ▢6 ounces sugar palm fruit kaong, drained
  • Ingredient 3 ▢12 ounces coconut gel nata de coco, drained
  • Ingredient 4 ▢2 cans 15 ounces each fruit cocktail, drained
  • Ingredient 5 ▢8 ounces pineapple chunks drained
  • Ingredient 6 ▢1 14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk
  • Ingredient 7 ▢7 ounces table cream

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 In a mixing bowl, combine young coconut, kaong, nata de coco,
  2. Step 2 pineapple chunks, and fruit cocktail.
  3. Step 3 Gently stir to distribute the ingredients.
  4. Step 4 Add sweetened condensed milk and table cream.
  5. Step 5 Mix until all the ingredients are properly distributed.
  6. Step 6 Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or place in the freezer for 1 hour.
  7. Step 7 Transfer to a serving bowl.Serve for dessert.Share and enjoy!

LECHE FLAN

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1▢10 pieces eggs
  • Ingredient 2▢1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  • Ingredient 3▢1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • Ingredient 4▢1 cup granulated sugar
  • Ingredient 5▢1 cup granulated sugar

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Using all the eggs, separate the yolk from the egg white (only egg yolks will be used).
  2. Step 2 Place the egg yolks in a big bowl then beat them using a fork or an egg beater
  3. Step 3 Add the condensed milk and mix thoroughly
  4. Step 4 Put the mold (llanera) on top of the stove and heat using low fire
  5. Step 5 Put-in the granulated sugar on the mold and mix
  6. Step 6 thoroughly until the solid sugar turns into liquid (caramel) having a light brown color.
  7. Step 7 Note: Sometimes it is hard to find a Llanera
  8. (Traditional flan mold) depending on your location.
  9. Step 8 I find it more convenient to use individual Round Pans in making leche flan.
  10. Step 9 Spread the caramel (liquid sugar) evenly on the flat side of the mold Wait for 5 minutes
  11. Step 10 pour the egg yolk and milk mixture on the mold
  12. Step 11 Cover the top of the mold using an Aluminum foil
  13. Step 12 Steam the mold with egg and milk mixture for 30 to 35 minutes
  14. Step 13 After steaming, let the temperature cool down then refrigerate
  15. Step 14 Serve for dessert. Share and Enjoy!

SUMAN

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢2 cups Glutinous rice (Malagkit)
  • Ingredient 2 ▢1 tsp. salt
  • Ingredient 3 ▢2 cups fresh or 1 canned coconut milk (400 ml
  • Ingredient 4 ▢ banana leaves for wrapping
  • Ingredient 5 ▢ water for boiling
  • Ingredient 6 ▢1 cup sugar (brown or white
  • Ingredient 7 ▢ If you are making latik (caramelized coconut cream)
  • Ingredient 8 ▢1 can coconut milk
  • Ingredient 9 ▢1 cup brown sugar
  • Ingredient 10 ▢1/2 tsp. salt

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Soak the glutinous rice (malagkit) in water for 2 to 3 hrs and then drain well
  2. Step 2 Meanwhile, prepare banana leaves by cutting it into 10 by 10 (inches) sheets and trim stiff ends.
  3. Step 3 Briefly pass the leaves over flames for 10 to 20 seconds.
  4. Step 4 Set aside.In a pot, mix coconut milk, salt, and sugar on medium heat and stir well.
  5. Step 5 Add glutinous rice and cook for 15 minutes. Stir occasionally until liquid is absorbed.
  6. Step 6 Remove from heat and allow to cool. Put 2 tablespoons on banana leaf.
  7. Step 7 Roll leaves tightly. Leave 1 inch on top and bottom side. Fold edges to seal and tie with a string.
  8. Step 8 In a steamer, stack the suman and steam for around 30 minutes to one hour over boiling water.
  9. Step 9 Make sure they are tender before removing them from the steamer.
  10. Step 10 Allow it to cool before unwrapping so that it will firm up.
  11. Step 11 Serve with sugar, sweetened grated coconut, latik or ripe mango. Share & enjoy!
  12. Step 12 If you are making latik (caramelized coconut cream)
  13. Step 13 In a pan, mix coconut milk, brown sugar and salt.
  14. Step 14 Stir well and cook on medium heat and bring to a boil until well mixed.
  15. Step 15 Turn of heat and serve.

MAJA BLANCA

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢4 cups coconut milk
  • Ingredient 2 ▢3/4 cup cornstarch
  • Ingredient 3 ▢14 ounces condensed milk
  • Ingredient 4 ▢3/4 cup fresh milk
  • Ingredient 5 ▢3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Ingredient 6 ▢15 ounces whole sweet kernel corn
  • Ingredient 7 ▢5 tbsp toasted grated coconut

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Pour the coconut milk in a cooking pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Step 2 Add the sugar, condensed milk, and whole sweet kernel corn then stir
  3. Step 3 until all the ingredients are evenly distributed.Simmer for 8 minutes
  4. Step 4 Combine the milk and cornstarch then whisk until the cornstarch is diluted
  5. Step 5 Pour the fresh milk and cornstarch mixture in the cooking pot and stir thoroughly.
  6. Step 6 Allow to cook while stirring until the mixture reaches your desired thickness
  7. Step 7 Pour the mixture in a serving tray then arrange
  8. Step 8 flatten the top using a flat tool such as a wooden spatula
  9. Step 9 Allow to cool down then refrigerate for at least 1 hour
  10. Step 10 Garnish with toasted grated coconut (or latik if available) Serve cold. Share and enjoy!
