CHOP SUEY

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢7 pieces shrimp cleaned and deveined
  • Ingredient 2 ▢3 ounces pork sliced
  • Ingredient 3 ▢3 ounces boneless chicken breast sliced
  • Ingredient 4 ▢1 ½ cup cauliflower florets
  • Ingredient 5 ▢1 piece carrot sliced crosswise into thin pieces
  • Ingredient 6 ▢15 pieces snow peas
  • Ingredient 7 ▢8 pieces baby corn
  • Ingredient 8 ▢1 piece red bell pepper sliced into squares
  • Ingredient 9 ▢1 piece green bell pepper sliced into squares
  • Ingredient 10 ▢1 ½ cups cabbage chopped
  • Ingredient 11 ▢12 pieces quail eggs boiled
  • Ingredient 12 ▢1 piece yellow onion sliced
  • Ingredient 13 ▢4 cloves garlic crushed
  • Ingredient 14 ▢¼ cup soy sauce
  • Ingredient 15 ▢1 ½ tablespoons oyster sauce
  • Ingredient 16 ▢¾ cup water
  • Ingredient 17 ▢1 tablespoon cornstarch diluted in ½ cup water
  • Ingredient 18 ▢¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Ingredient 19 ▢3 tablespoons cooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Heat oil in a wok or pan.
  2. Step 2 Pan fry the shrimp for 1 minute per side.
  3. Step 3 Remove from the wok. Set aside.
  4. Step 4 Saute onion. Add garlic and continue to saute until the onion becomes soft.
  5. Step 5 Add pork and chicken. Stir fry until light brown.
  6. Step 6 Add soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir.
  7. Step 7 Pour water. Let boil. Cover and cook in medium heat for 15 minutes.
  8. Step 8 Add cauliflower, carrots, bell peppers, snow peas, and young corn. Stir.
  9. Step 9 Add cabbage. Toss. Cover and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
  10. Step 10 Put the pan-fried shrimp into the pot and add ground black pepper.
  11. Step 11 Add the boiled quail eggs and cornstarch diluted in water. Toss.
  12. Step 12 Transfer to a serving plate. Serve.Share and enjoy!

PAKBET

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢1 lb lechon kawali sliced
  • Ingredient 2 ▢1 piece Knorr Shrimp Cube
  • Ingredient 3 ▢12 pieces sitaw cut into 2 inch length
  • Ingredient 4 ▢1/2 piece kalabasa cubed
  • Ingredient 5 ▢12 pieces okra
  • Ingredient 6 ▢1 piece Chinese eggplant sliced
  • Ingredient 7 ▢1 piece ampalaya sliced
  • Ingredient 8 ▢▢1 piece kamote cubed (optional)
  • Ingredient 9 ▢2 pieces tomato cubed
  • Ingredient 10 ▢2 thumbs ginger crushed (optional)
  • Ingredient 11 ▢1 piece onion chopped
  • Ingredient 12 ▢4 cloves garlic crushed
  • Ingredient 13 ▢2 teaspoons bagoong alamang
  • Ingredient 14 ▢2 ½ cups water
  • Ingredient 15 ▢3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Ingredient 16 ▢¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Heat cooking oil in a pot. Saute onion and garlic.
  2. Step 2 Add ginger and continue to cook until the onion softens.
  3. Step 3 Add lechon kawali. Saute for 1 minute.Pour water. Let boil.
  4. Step 4 Add Knorr Shrimp Cube. Cover the pot and cook in medium heat for 20 minutes.
  5. Step 5 Add tomato and bagoong alamang. Stir. Cover the pot. Cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Step 6 Put the kalabasa and kamote into the pot. Cook for 7 minutes.
  7. Step 7 Add sitaw, okra, ampalaya, and eggplant. Stir. Cover the pot and cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Step 8 Season with ground black pepper and add remaining lechon kawali. Cook for minutes.
  9. Step 9 Tranfer to a serving plate. Serve. Share and Enjoy!

  • Ingredient 4 ▢2 cups spinach (or alugbati)
  • Ingredient 5 ▢1 piece tomato chopped
  • Ingredient 6 ▢1 piece onion chopped
  • Ingredient 7 ▢2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Ingredient 8 ▢24 ounces water for boiling
  • Ingredient 9 ▢1 piece Knorr beef cube for flavoring
  • Ingredient 10 ▢1/2 cup crushed pork rind chicharon
  • Ingredient 11 ▢1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 In a pan, put-in the water and bring to a boil
  2. Step 2 Put-in the Mung beans and simmer until becomes soft (about 35 to 50 minutes)
  3. Step 3 On a separate pan, sauté the garlic,onion, and tomato.
  4. Step 4 Add the pork. Cook for 5 mins
  5. Step 5 Put-in the beef cube and fish sauce. Simmer for 10 mins or until the meat is tender.
  6. Step 6 Note: If necessary, you may add water to help make the meat tender but make sure
  7. Step 7 to add more time to simmer Add the shrimp. Stir and then cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Step 8 Pour the cooked Mung beans. Stir and then simmer for 10 minutes
  9. Step 9 Add the spinach and pork rinds (chicharon)
  10. Step 10 Sprinkle the ground black pepper
  11. Step 11 Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!

GINATAANG LANGKA

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢20 oz. unripe jackfruit chopped
  • Ingredient 2 ▢6 ounces pork shoulder sliced into thin pieces
  • Ingredient 3 ▢2 packs Knorr Ginataang Gulay Recipe Mix 33g per pack
  • Ingredient 4 ▢1 piece onion chopped
  • Ingredient 5 ▢3 pieces Thai chili pepper chopped
  • Ingredient 6 ▢3 cloves garlic crushed
  • Ingredient 7 ▢3 cloves garlic crushed
  • Ingredient 8 ▢3 tablespoons cooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Combine Knorr Ginataang Gulay Recipe Mix with 2 cups water.
  2. Step 2 Mix well. Set aside.
  3. Step 3 Heat oil in a cooking pot. Saute onion and garlic.
  4. Step 4 Add pork slices once onion softens.
  5. Step 5 Continue to saute for 3 to 5 minutes until the pork turns medium brown and gets completely cooked.
  6. Step 6 Add unripe jackfruit. Cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Step 7 Put the chopped chili in the pot. Stir and cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Step 8 Pour the coconut milk mixture.
  9. Step 9 Let boil. Continue to cook uncovered until the sauce reduces to a quarter.
  10. Step 10 Transfer to a serving bowl. Share and enjoy!

ADOBONG SITAW

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢1 ½ lb pork belly cut into serving pieces
  • Ingredient 2 ▢10 pieces snake beans sitaw, cut into 2 inch length pieces
  • Ingredient 3 ▢1 medium onion
  • Ingredient 4 ▢5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Ingredient 5 ▢2 ½ tablespoons vinegar
  • Ingredient 6 ▢5 cloves garlic crushed
  • Ingredient 7 ▢1 ½ tablespoons brown sugar
  • Ingredient 8 ▢½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Ingredient 9 ▢2 cups beef broth
  • Ingredient 10 ▢3 tablespoons cooking oil

INGREDIENTS:

  1. Step 1 Combine pork, soy sauce, ground black pepper, and garlic. Mix well.
  2. Step 2 Let it stay for 15 minutes.Heat oil in a cooking pot.
  3. Step 3 Sauté the onion until it gets soft.
  4. Step 4 Add the marinated pork including the remaining soy sauce and garlic.
  5. Step 5 Cook for 5 minutes in medium heat.
  6. Step 6 Pour the beef broth in the pot. Let boil. .
  7. Step 7 Cook in low heat for 45 minutes or until the pork gets tender
  8. Step 8 Note: add more beef broth or water if the liquid starts to dry out.
  9. Step 9 Add the vinegar. Allow the liquid to re-boil. Stir.
  10. Step 10 Put the string beans in the pot. Cook for 3 minutes.
  11. Step 11 Add brown sugar. Stir. Continue to cook for 5 to 8 minutes.
  12. Step 12 Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve!Share and enjoy!
