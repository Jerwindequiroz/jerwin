HAM AND CHEESE ROLLS

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢8 slices bread
  • Ingredient 2 ▢4 ounces ham sliced into thick strips
  • Ingredient 3 ▢3 ounces cheese sliced into thick strips
  • Ingredient 4 ▢1/4 cup pimento optional
  • Ingredient 5 ▢3/4 cup Panko bread crumbs
  • Ingredient 6 ▢2 pieces eggs beaten
  • Ingredient 7 ▢2 cups cooking oil
  • Ingredient 8 ▢1/2 cup mayonnaise

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Remove the dark ends of the sliced bread.
  2. Step 2 Heat the cooking oil in a cooking pot.
  3. Step 3 Place the bread slices in a zip lock bag.
  4. Step 4 Flatten using a rolling pin.
  5. Step 5 Spread mayonnaise on the flatten piece of bread
  6. Step 6 Arrange the ham, cheese, and pimento strips on the bread.
  7. Step 7 Roll the bread until the filling is covered.
  8. Step 8 Secure with a toothpick.
  9. Step 9 Dip the rolled bread in beaten egg.
  10. Step 10 Place the Panko bread crumbs in a large Ziploc bag
  11. Step 11 And then put the dipped rolls in.
  12. Step 12 Shake until everything is covered with crumbs.
  13. Step 13 Fry in low to medium heat until the color turns golden brown (about 5 to 7 minutes).
  14. Step 14 Arrange the fried rolls in a plate line with paper towels.
  15. Step 15 Serve as a snack or slice into pieces and serve as an appetizer.Share and enjoy!

ATCHARANG PAPAYA

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢3 lbs shredded green papaya
  • Ingredient 2 ▢1 medium carrot thinly sliced
  • Ingredient 3 ▢1 small red bell pepper julienne
  • Ingredient 4 ▢3/4 cups raisins
  • Ingredient 5 ▢1 head garlic sliced
  • Ingredient 6 ▢4 thumbs ginger julienne
  • Ingredient 7 ▢2 cups vinegar
  • Ingredient 8 ▢1 1/3 cups granulated white sugar
  • Ingredient 9 ▢1 teaspoon salt
  • Ingredient 10 ▢1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Ingredient 11 Soak Tha Papaya In
  • Ingredient 12 ▢5 cups water
  • Ingredient 13 ▢4 tablespoons salt

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Combine water and 4 tablespoons salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. Step 2 Add the shredded papaya.
  3. Step 3 Cover and place inside the refrigerator. Soak for 2 days.
  4. Step 4 Put the papaya in a cheesecloth in a way that it is trapped in the center.
  5. Step 5 Gently rotate the cheesecloth while tightly holding one end.
  6. Step 6 Continue to rotate until the papaya completely releases liquid.
  7. Step 7 Do this more than once for better results.
  8. Step 8 Arrange the papaya in a mixing bowl.
  9. Step 9 Add all the vegetables and raisin. Toss and set aside.
  10. Step 10 Make the syrup by heating the vinegar in a cooking pot.
  11. Step 11 Once it starts to boil, add sugar and 1 teaspoon salt.
  12. Step 12 Mix well and cook for 1 to 3 minutes.
  13. Step 13 Put the papaya mixture in a mason jar.
  14. Step 14 Do not completely fill the jar, leave a little space for the syrup.
  15. Step 15 Gently pour the hot syrup in each jar. Quickly close the lid.
  16. Step 16 This will somewhat cook the papaya and veggies and will make it a bit softer.
  17. Step 17 Let it cool and then refrigerate, or simply store in a shelf in room temperature.

SIZZLING SISIG

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢1 ½ lbs. pork belly boiled for 1 hour
  • Ingredient 2 ▢½ cup Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise
  • Ingredient 3 ▢2 pieces onion chopped
  • Ingredient 4 ▢4 pieces Thai chili pepper chopped
  • Ingredient 5 ▢2 pieces long green pepper chopped
  • Ingredient 6 ▢16 grams Knorr SavorRich Pork equivalent to 1 sachet
  • Ingredient 7 ▢½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Ingredient 8 ▢2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Ingredient 9 ▢¼ cup margarine
  • Ingredient 10 ▢2 cups cooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Heat oil in a deep cooking pot or deep fryer.
  2. Step 2 Fry the boiled pork belly until golden brown and crispy
  3. Step 3 Chop the crispy pork belly into small pieces. Set aside.
  4. Step 4 Heat half of the margarine in a pan.
  5. Step 5 Sauté half of the onions until soft.
  6. Step 6 Add chopped pork. Saute for 30 seconds.
  7. Step 7 Add Knorr SavorRich. Stir and then add chili peppers and season with
  8. Step 8 ground black pepper and soy sauce.
  9. Step 9 Cook for 1 minute.Add Lady’s Choice Mayonnaise.
  10. Step 10 Stir and add remaining onion. Set aside.
  11. Step 11 Heat a metal plate (sizzling plate) on a stovetop. Melt remaining margarine.
  12. Step 12 Transfer the crispy pork sisig into the metal plate. Let it stay for 30 seconds
  13. Step 13 Serve with warm rice and your favorite beverage. Enjoy!.

LUMPIANG SHANGHAI

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢2 1/2 lbs. ground pork
  • Ingredient 2 ▢1 piece Knorr Pork Cube
  • Ingredient 3 ▢50 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • Ingredient 4 ▢3/4 cup carrot minced
  • Ingredient 5 ▢2 pieces onions minced
  • Ingredient 6 ▢2 pieces eggs
  • Ingredient 7 ▢¼ cup parsley minced
  • Ingredient 8 ▢½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Ingredient 9 ▢1 teaspoon salt
  • Ingredient 10 ▢1 teaspoon Sesame oil
  • Ingredient 11 ▢1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Ingredient 12 ▢2 cups cooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Combine ground pork, carrot, onion, and parsley in a bowl. Mix well.
  2. Step 2 Add egg, ground black pepper, salt, garlic, powder, and Sesame oil.
  3. Step 3 Continue to mix until the ingredients are well blended.
  4. Step 4 Crush the Knorr pork cube and add it into the mixture.
  5. Step 5 Blend the ingredients altogether completely.
  6. Step 6 Put 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons of meat mixture over a piece of lumpia wrapper.
  7. Step 7 Wrap and roll to secure the mixture.
  8. Step 8 Continue this step until the mixture is completely consumed.
  9. Step 9 Heat oil in a cooking pot in low to medium heat.
  10. Step 10 Deep fry the lumpia until it turns golden brown.
  11. Step 11 Remove from the pot and place in a plate lined with paper towel.
  12. Step 12 Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with sweet and sour sauce. Share and enjoy!

BUFFALO WINGS

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢2 lbs chicken wings
  • Ingredient 2 ▢5 Tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Ingredient 3 ▢1/2 cup Frank's RedHot

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Divide the wings into 3 parts. Separate the drumette from the wingette.
  2. Step 2 Cut off the wingtip and set aside. Note: we'll only be using the big parts.
  3. Step 3 Pat the wings dry with paper towel and arrange inside an air fryer.
  4. Step 4 Air fry one side for 12 minutes at 350F.
  5. Step 5 Do the same with the opposite side.
  6. Step 6 Make the sauce by melting butter in a microwave oven.
  7. Step 7 Add the hot sauce and stir until well blended.
  8. Step 8 Soak chicken wings in the sauce. Make sure that each piece is fully coated.
  9. Step 9 Serve with ranch dressing, carrots, and celery. Share and enjoy!.
