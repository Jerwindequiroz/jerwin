BULALO

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢2 lbs beef shank
  • Ingredient 2 ▢½ cabbage whole leaf individually detached
  • Ingredient 3 ▢1 bundle bok choy
  • Ingredient 4 ▢2 cobs corn each cut into 3 parts
  • Ingredient 5 ▢2 Tablespoons whole peppercorn
  • Ingredient 6 ▢1/2 cup green onions cut unto 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • Ingredient 7 ▢1 onion
  • Ingredient 8 ▢34 ounces water
  • Ingredient 9 ▢fish sauce to taste optional

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 In a big cooking pot, pour in water and bring to a boil
  2. Step 2 Put-in the beef shank followed by the onion and whole pepper corn
  3. Step 3 then simmer for 1.5 hours (30 mins if using a pressure cooker) or until meat is tender.
  4. Step 4 Add the corn and simmer for another 10 minutes
  5. Step 5 Add the fish sauce,cabbage, pechay, and green onion (onion leeks)
  6. Step 6 Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!

CREAMY CHICKEN SOPAS

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢12 oz. chicken breasts boneless
  • Ingredient 2 ▢½ piece cabbage sliced
  • Ingredient 3 ▢3 stalks celery chopped
  • Ingredient 4 ▢1 piece onion diced
  • Ingredient 5 ▢3 pieces hotdog sliced
  • Ingredient 6 ▢¾ cups carrots diced
  • Ingredient 7 ▢12 oz. evaporated milk
  • Ingredient 8 ▢1 ½ cups elbow macaroni
  • Ingredient 9 ▢2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • Ingredient 10 ▢1 piece Knorr Chicken Cube
  • Ingredient 11 ▢6 to 8 cups water
  • Ingredient 12 ▢3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Ingredient 13 ▢Salt and ground black pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Boil 3 cups water in a medium pot. Add the chicken breasts.
  2. Step 2 Cover and cook in medium heat for 15 to 18 minutes.
  3. Step 3 You may add more water if needed.
  4. Step 4 Separate the chicken from the water.Let the chicken cool down and then shred
  5. Step 5 Set both shredded chicken and water aside.Heat oil in a large soup pot.
  6. Step 6 Sauté the garlic until it turns medium brown.
  7. Step 7 Add onion, celery, and carrot. Continue to sauté for 2 to 3 minutes.
  8. Step 8 Add the shredded chicken. Stir and cook for 1 minute.
  9. Step 9 Add the hot dog.Quickly stir and then pour the water used to boil the chicken.
  10. Step 10 Also pour the remaining water.Let boil.Add the Knorr Chicken Cube.
  11. Step 11 Stir. Cover and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.Put the elbow macaroni in the pot.
  12. Step 12 Cook for 10 minutes.Add the cabbage. Stir and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
  13. Step 13 Note: you can add more water if needed.
  14. Step 14 Pour the evaporated milk. Stirlet boil, cover and cook for another 3 minutes.
  15. Step 15 Add salt and ground black pepper to taste.
  16. Step 16 Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve.Share and enjoy!

ARROZ CALDO

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢1½ lbs chicken cut into serving pieces
  • Ingredient 2 ▢1½ cups rice uncooked
  • Ingredient 3 ▢34 ounces water about 1 liter
  • Ingredient 4 ▢2 Tablespoons fish sauce
  • Ingredient 5 ▢1 teaspoon garlic
  • Ingredient 6 ▢1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Ingredient 7 ▢1 cup onion minced
  • Ingredient 8 ▢4 eggs hard boiled
  • Ingredient 9 ▢1 cup scallions green onions, minced
  • Ingredient 10 ▢2 knobs ginger julienned
  • Ingredient 11 ▢3 tbsp safflower kasubha
  • Ingredient 12 ▢1 chicken cube bouillon
  • Ingredient 13 ▢1 lemon or 4 pieces calamansi
  • Ingredient 14 ▢2 Tablespoon cooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 In a pot, heat the cooking oil then saute the garlic, onion, and ginger
  2. Step 2 Dash-in some ground black pepper
  3. Step 3 Add the chicken cube and cook until the cube melts
  4. Step 4 Put-in the chicken and cook until outer layer color turns golden brown
  5. Step 5 Add the fish sauce and uncooked rice then mix and cook for a few minutes
  6. Step 6 Pour-in the water and bring to a boil
  7. Step 7 Stir occasionally and simmer until the rice is fully cooked (about 30 to 40 minutes)
  8. Step 8 Put-in the hard boiled eggs
  9. Step 9 Add the safflower for additional color and aroma
  10. Step 10 Serve hot with garlic, minced scallions, and lemon. Share and Enjoy!

LOMI

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢1/2 lb. fresh flat miki noodles
  • Ingredient 2 ▢10 ounces boneless chicken breast sliced into thin pieces
  • Ingredient 3 ▢1 Knorr Chicken Cube
  • Ingredient 4 ▢1/2 medium cabbage chopped
  • Ingredient 5 ▢1 cup chopped carrots
  • Ingredient 6 ▢1/2 cup chopped scallions
  • Ingredient 7 ▢1 medium yellow onion cubed
  • Ingredient 8 ▢3 cloves garlic crushed
  • Ingredient 9 ▢1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Ingredient 10 ▢1 egg
  • Ingredient 11 ▢6 to 7 cups water
  • Ingredient 12 ▢1 teaspoon salt
  • Ingredient 13 ▢1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Ingredient 14 ▢3 tablespoons cooking oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Heat oil in a cooking pot.
  2. Step 2 Saute garlic, onion, and carrots.
  3. Step 3 Add the sliced chicken. Cook until the chicken turns light brown.
  4. Step 4 Pour the water into the pot. Let boil.
  5. Step 5 Add Knorr chicken cube. Stir. Cover the pot.
  6. Step 6 Cook the chicken between low to medium heat for 15 minutes.
  7. Step 7 Add cabbage and miki noodles. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
  8. Step 8 Add scallions. Stir
  9. Step 9 Pour the egg. Continuously stir to prevent the egg from forming.
  10. Step 10 Add 1/4 cup water to cornstarch and stir to mix. Pour it into the pot.
  11. Step 11 Stir until the soup thickens.
  12. Step 12 Add salt and ground black pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.
  13. Step 13 Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve.
  14. Step 14 Share and enjoy!

CHAMPORADO

INGREDIENTS:

  • Ingredient 1 ▢8 tbsp cocoa powder or about 4 pieces tableya
  • Ingredient 2 ▢1 cup glutinous rice malagkit
  • Ingredient 3 ▢1/2 cup sugar
  • Ingredient 4 ▢3 1/2 cups water
  • Ingredient 5 ▢condensed milk optional

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Step 1 Pour 2 1/2 cups of water in a pot and bring to a boil
  2. Step 2 Put-in the glutinous rice and allow water to re-boil for a few minutes
  3. Step 3 Dilute the cocoa powder in 1 cup warm water then pour-in the pot.Stir continuously
  4. Step 4 Once the glutinous rice is cooked (about 12 to 18 minutes of cooking with constant stirring),
  5. Step 5 add the sugar and cook for another 5 minutes or until the texture becomes thick.
  6. Step 6 Remove from the pot and place in a serving bowl.
  7. Step 7 Serve hot with a swirl of condensed milk on top.
